### Design System

#### Colors
- **Primary**: Blue (#2563EB)
- **Secondary**: Purple (#9333EA)
- **Accent**: Indigo (#4F46E5)
- **Success**: Green (#10B981)
- **Warning**: Orange (#F97316)
- **Background**: Slate/Blue gradient

#### Typography
- **Headings**: Bold, clear hierarchy
- **Body**: Regular weight, comfortable reading
- **Labels**: Semibold, uppercase tracking

#### Components
- **Cards**: 2xl rounded corners, 2px borders
- **Shadows**: Layered, professional
- **Hover**: Scale, translate, shadow transitions
- **Icons**: HeroIcons 24px outline
